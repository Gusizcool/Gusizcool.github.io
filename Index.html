Recipe #1

Cheese Pizza

  • Time: 15min
  • Serves: 8 slices
Ingredients Quantity
Mozzarella Your Choice
Pizza Sauce Your Choice
Pizza Dough Your Choice
Parmesan Cheese Your Choice

Step 1: Stretch your pizza dough into a 12 in circle on a slightly floured surface
Step 2: Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving a 1 inch gap for the crust
Step 3: Sprinkle shreaded mozzarella onto the pie
Step 4: Sprinkle with parmesan before baking for 12 to 14 minutes, or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling

Recipe #2

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 20min
  • Serves: 12 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Brown sugar 1/2 cup
White sugar 1/4 cup
Butter Your Choice
Flour Your Choice
Vanilla extract Your Choice
Egg 1
Table salt 1/4 teaspoon
Chocolate chips Your Choice
Baking soda Your Choice

Step 1: You’re going to start by melting the butter. The butter shouldn’t be sizzling hot. If you’re not sure how hot is too hot, you’ll want it about 90% melted, and then you’ll whisk it until it finishes melting.
Step 2: Once the butter has melted, whisk in the sugars until well-combined.
Step 3: Mix in the vanilla extract and the egg.
Step 4: Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in just until combined. You don’t want to overmix the dough.
Step 5: Stir in the chocolate chips, and then scoop the dough. Place about 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheet.
Step 6: Bake until the cookies are set on the edges but slightly underdone in the middle, about 7-10 minutes. Slightly under-baking the cookies helps to keep them soft.

Source: Easiest Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Recipe #3

Vanilla Ice Cream

  • Time: 5min
  • Serves: 6 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Heavy cream 1 3/4 cups
Whole milk 1 1/4 cups
Sugar 3/4 cup
Fine sea salt 1/8 teaspoon
Vanilla extract 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Pour 1 cup of the cream into a saucepan and add the sugar, salt. Scrape the seeds of the vanilla bean into the pot and then add the vanilla pod to the pot.
Step 2: Warm the mixture over medium heat, just until the sugar dissolves. Remove from the heat and add the remaining cream, milk, and vanilla. Stir to combine and chill in the refrigerator.
Step 3: When ready to churn, remove the vanilla pod, whisk mixture again and pour into ice cream maker. Churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
Step 4: Transfer the finished ice cream to an airtight container and place in the freezer until ready to serve.

Source: The Best (and Easiest) Ice Cream You’ll Ever Make
